❓ Help

To login on other devices without this extension:

1. Use an online SHA256 generator
2. Enter: your_password + domain_name
3. Generate the hash
4. Add B.2 at the end of the hash
5. That's your password!

Example: If your password is "secret" and domain is "example.com", hash "secretexample.com", take only first 20 characters and append "B.2"

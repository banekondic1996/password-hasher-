Enter your password in password field, press "Hash Password" to hash the password.

Generated password is your password + domain name, hashed to SHA256 and "B.2" added at the end, because of usual requirement of at least 1 capital letter and 1 symbol.

To login on other devices without this extension:

1. Use an online SHA256 generator
2. Enter: your_password + domain_name
3. Generate the hash
4. Add B.2 at the end of the hash
5. That's your password!

Example: If your password is "secret" and domain is "example.com", hash "secretexample.com" and append "B.2"
